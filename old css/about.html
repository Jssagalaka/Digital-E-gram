"We assist in sustaining rural development through technology"

The ‘Digital Gram Panchayat’ is a software developed by Akeria Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. The software is essential to support Digital India making Rural people's life easy and hassle-free, and helping them in development.

The Gram Panchayat software is specially developed for Gram Panchayat to support Digital India. It helps you to run all Gram Panchayat's operations smoothly and makes the process very easy. All data is safe and secured in the software, and helps you to operate anywhere as it supports web - based and mobile applications. All important documents and processes are hassle - free such as tax collection processes, birth, marriage, death certificates, various bill request and many other types of all certificates which can be issued & generated by Gram Panchayat as per government format.

A click is all it takes to manage the software with a simple user - friendly interface, and to streamline operations smoothly.
